Health Insurance Risk Calculator

Welcome to the Health Insurance Risk Calculator!

Follow these steps to calculate your health risk:

  1. Enter your age (years).
  2. Enter your height in centimeters (minimum of 60 cm).
  3. Enter your weight in kilograms.
  4. Enter your blood pressure (systolic and diastolic).
  5. Select any family history of diseases such as Diabetes, Cancer, or Alzheimer’s.
  6. Click on "Calculate Risk" to get your result!

Note: Make sure all fields are filled correctly before submitting the form.
